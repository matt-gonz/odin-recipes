High Calorie Protein Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cup oat milk
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 scoop protein powder, 25g protein/scoop
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 2 cup oats, old fashioned
  • 2/3 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 large banana

Description

This is a quick and simple way for you to get your calories in while on a "bulk". Great for those of you that struggle to hit you macro targets through eating regular meals, as this allows you to instead drink your calories.

***Adjust amounts as needed to fit your individual macronutrient needs.***

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients together in a blender until smooth.
