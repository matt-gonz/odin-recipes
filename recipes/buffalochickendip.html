Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 can chunk chicken breast, drained
  • frank's red hot sauce

Description

Enjoy this versatile dip with tortilla chips, tostadas, or even use it as a filling for your quesadillas.

Steps

  1. Heat butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add in cream cheese, and then stir in chicken.
  2. Break the chunks of chicken apart, and then sprinkle in the shredded cheese. Pour in frank's red hot sauce to taste. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
